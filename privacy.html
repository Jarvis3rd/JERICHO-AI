This Privacy Policy explains how Aperture Automations LLC, a California limited liability company (“Aperture Automations,” “we,” “us,” or “our”), collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our website and our AI agent services, including the Jericho voice agent and our messaging, email, and scheduling automation.

1.Who we are

Aperture Automations is an AI automation provider based in Fresno, California, offering managed AI voice agents and related automation to businesses across the United States. You can reach us at info@apertureautomations.ai or P.O. Box 26514, Fresno, CA 93729.

2.Scope of this policy

This policy covers two distinct contexts:

  • Our website — information from visitors who browse the site or book a call with us.
  • Our services — information processed when we operate AI agents on behalf of a business client. For that client data, we generally act as a service provider / processor on the client’s behalf, and the client’s own privacy notice and our service agreement govern the details.

3.Information we collect

Information you give us

When you book a demo, we use Calendly to schedule the call. Through that booking you may provide your name, email address, phone number, and any details you choose to share. If you email us, we receive your message and contact details.

Information processed through our services

When we run an AI agent for a client, the agent may handle communications from that client’s customers and prospects. Depending on the configuration, this can include names, phone numbers, email addresses, the content of calls and messages, scheduling details, and information synced with the client’s CRM or calendar.

Information collected automatically

Our website does not use advertising or analytics tracking cookies. Our hosting provider may keep standard server logs (such as IP address and request time) for security and reliability.

4.How we use information

  • To respond to inquiries, schedule and conduct demo calls, and provide our services.
  • To build, voice, deploy, and continuously improve AI agents configured for each client.
  • To route qualified leads, log interactions, and integrate with the tools a client has authorized.
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

5.Call handling and recording

When we operate an AI voice agent, calls are processed in real time so the agent can understand and respond. Depending on the client’s configuration, calls may be recorded, transcribed, and summarized to deliver and improve the service. Where calls are recorded, all parties are notified at the start of the call, consistent with California’s all-party consent law and similar laws in other states.

6.How we share information

We do not sell personal information. We share information only as needed to operate our services, with trusted vendors (“subprocessors”) acting on our instructions. Depending on the service, these may include:

  • OpenAI — language and real-time voice processing
  • LiveKit — real-time voice transport
  • mem0 — agent memory
  • Resend — transactional email and message delivery
  • Google — calendar and email integration, where authorized by a client
  • Calendly — demo scheduling
  • GoHighLevel — CRM and workflow automation
  • Hosting provider — secure hosting of our infrastructure

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, users, or the public.
