Is there really a service that waits on hold for me?

Yes. Concierge Caller calls the company for you, waits through the hold music and phone menus, and rings you back the moment a real person picks up. You never sit on hold again.

How does Concierge Caller work?

You tell us who to call and what you need. We dial in, navigate the phone menus, and hold the line. When a live human answers, your phone rings and you're connected instantly — straight to a person.

Do I have to stay by my phone while it waits?

Nope. Go live your life. Concierge Caller does all the waiting and only rings you when there's a real human on the line ready to talk.

Which companies can it call?

Most major companies with phone support — utilities, insurance, airlines, banks, internet providers, government agencies, and more. If they put people on hold, we can wait for you.

What if a real person is never reached?

Then you're not charged. No human, no charge — you only pay when we actually connect you to a live person.

How much does it cost?

Simple and fair: your first call is free. After that, it's $15 per successful call — and you're only charged when we actually connect you to a real person. No human, no charge.
